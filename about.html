About Us

Our Commitment to Your Health

Providing state-of-the-art medical treatments with standard guidelines and compassionate support since 1998.

Our Journey to Becoming a Trusted Healthcare Leader

Founded in 1998, Seva Multi Specialist Hospital started with a humble vision: to make advanced medical healthcare accessible to everyone with transparency and empathy. Over the last 28 years, we have grown from a local clinic into a nationally accredited multi-specialty institution with 200+ beds and a dedicated staff of over 300 healthcare workers.

We focus on constant technological evolution. Our diagnostic labs are NABL-accredited, and we are proud to offer state-of-the-art robotic surgical systems, minimally invasive procedures, and a highly responsive 24/7 ICU system.

At the heart of Seva Hospital is the belief of "Seva" (meaning selfless service). Our medical ethics and care processes ensure that every patient is treated with respect and receives personalized, evidence-based therapies.
