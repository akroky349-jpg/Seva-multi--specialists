Our Specialties

Clinical Departments

Expert medical assistance across eight core fields of specialist healthcare, powered by modern technology.

General Medicine Cardiology Orthopedics Gynecology Pediatrics Neurology Dermatology ENT

General Medicine

Our General Medicine department is the primary clinical team for preventive care, diagnoses, and non-surgical treatments of chronic conditions. We provide comprehensive outpatient checkups and manage acute illnesses with personalized follow-up care guidelines.

  • Preventive Health Exams
  • Chronic Disease Management
  • Infectious Disease Treatment
  • Elderly & Geriatric Care
Book Doctor consultation

Cardiology

As a premium cardiac care provider, the Cardiology department handles coronary angiographies, bypass surgeries, heart failure management, and valve repair. Our advanced cardiac lab operates 24/7 to address cardiac emergencies with minimally invasive interventions.

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG) & Echo
  • Coronary Angioplasty & Stenting
  • Pacemaker Implantations
  • Heart Rhythm Disorders (EP)
Book Doctor consultation

Orthopedics

Our Orthopedics department has an exceptional track record in joint replacements, sports injury treatments, ligament reconstructions, and spine correction. Supported by a specialized physiotherapy wing, we ensure early mobilization and complete recovery.

  • Hip & Knee Replacement
  • Arthroscopic Keyhole Surgery
  • Trauma and Complex Fractures
  • Spine & Disc Pain Therapy
Book Doctor consultation

Gynecology & Obstetrics

We provide compassionate care for women during all phases of life. From pre-pregnancy consultations and high-risk pregnancy deliveries to advanced keyhole gynecological surgeries, our maternal suites offer the utmost comfort and security.

  • Antenatal Care & Delivery
  • Laparoscopic Hysterectomy
  • Infertility Diagnostics
  • Menopausal Health Clinics
Book Doctor consultation

Pediatrics

Dedicated to infant, child, and adolescent health, our Pediatrics department is staffed with caring professionals. Our facility contains a Level 3 Neonatal ICU (NICU) to provide critical support for preterm infants.

  • Routine Immunization Plans
  • Level 3 Neonatal ICU (NICU)
  • Pediatric Emergency Service
  • Growth & Development Tracking
Book Doctor consultation

Neurology

Specializing in nervous system diseases, our neurologists treat stroke cases, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, dementia, and chronic migraines. Our neuro-diagnostic labs feature high-end EEG, EMG, and 3T MRI systems.

  • 24/7 Hyperacute Stroke Care
  • Video Electroencephalography (EEG)
  • Neuromuscular Electromyography (EMG)
  • Rehabilitation Therapies
Book Doctor consultation

Dermatology

We diagnose and treat skin, hair, and nail conditions. Our state-of-the-art clinical dermatology procedures address psoriasis, vitiligo, skin cancers, and cosmetic enhancements under professional supervision.

  • Psoriasis & Eczema Control
  • Medical Acne & Scar Therapy
  • Hair Fall & Scalp Cliniques
  • Laser Laser Treatments
Book Doctor consultation

ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat)

Our Ear, Nose & Throat (ENT) clinical specialists provide medical and surgical management of hearing losses, sinus issues, voice disorders, and sleep-apneas. We feature advanced endoscopic diagnostic setups for early, precise detections.

  • Hearing Aid Assessments
  • Endoscopic Sinus Surgery
  • Tonsillitis & Adenoid Care
  • Vertigo Diagnosis Clinic
